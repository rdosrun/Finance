Annuities and Perpetuities

Concept Formula Description
Present Value of Annuity (PVA) PV = PMT × [1 − (1 + r)−t] / r PV of equal payments over time
Future Value of Annuity (FVA) FV = PMT × [(1 + r)t − 1] / r FV of equal payments
Present Value of Perpetuity PV = PMT / r Infinite series of cash flows
