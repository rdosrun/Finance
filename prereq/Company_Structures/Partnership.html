Partnership

A partnership is a business structure where two or more individuals share ownership and operate the business together. All partners typically contribute to the business financially or through labor, and they share profits and losses according to a partnership agreement. This structure allows for shared responsibilities and resources but also means shared liability for debts and legal actions.

Pros:

  • Easy to establish with minimal formalities
  • Shared financial commitment and workload
  • Broader skill set and ideas from multiple partners
  • Income is taxed only once (passed through to personal tax returns)
  • Flexibility in roles and responsibilities

Cons:

  • Unlimited personal liability for general partners
  • Potential for conflicts between partners
  • Profits must be shared
  • Less stabilityâ€”business may dissolve if a partner leaves
  • Harder to transfer ownership
